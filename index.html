About

A bridge between
two markets

I am a final-year Business Management student at Oxford Brookes University, graduating May 2026, with a BBA from EU Business School Geneva. My goal is to join a Spanish private equity firm as an analyst — not for the name, but to learn from the inside how companies are built and transformed, before building something of my own.

What sets me apart is not a line on a CV — it is a lived understanding of the Spain–Morocco trade corridor. Born and raised in Melilla, native in both Spanish and Arabic, with direct family exposure to cross-border logistics in Nador, I bring something that cannot be learned in a classroom.

Morocco is an emerging market of 37 million people with a growing middle class, competitive production costs, EU trade agreements, and two hours from Madrid. For portfolio companies looking to expand into new markets or internationalise their supply chain, this corridor represents a real opportunity — and I can navigate it from day one.
